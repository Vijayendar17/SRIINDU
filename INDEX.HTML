TECHNOTSAV 2025

The Technotsav was successfully held on 22.02.2025. This event featured various technical and non-technical competitions, with enthusiastic participation from students both inside and outside the college.

The morning session was dedicated to technical events designed to test core engineering skills. The competition included:
**1. Resume Roast:** Participants presented their resumes to a judging panel, who offered constructive feedback on formatting, content, and professional presentation. This round prepared students to create impactful resumes for potential employers.
**2. Technical Assessment:** Participants faced a series of technical questions relevant to their fields, assessing their knowledge, problem-solving abilities, and critical thinking.

The competition culminated in a **Final HR Interview Simulation** round, where participants answered questions about their experiences, strengths, weaknesses, and career aspirations, providing valuable experience for real-world placement drives.

ENGINEER'S DAY CELEBRATIONS

National Engineers' Day is a special occasion celebrated India on 15th September each year. It is a day dedicated to recognizing and celebrating the remarkable contributions of engineers across various fields. The celebrations often include technical talks, workshops, and student competitions.

Engineers are hailed for their spirit of innovation, problem-solving abilities, and their pivotal role in shaping the world as we know it. This day holds immense significance as it commemorates the birth anniversary of one of India's greatest engineers and visionaries, Sir Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya, who made monumental contributions to irrigation and flood control.
